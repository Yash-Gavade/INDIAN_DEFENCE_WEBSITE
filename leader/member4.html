Ram Nath Kovind (pronunciation (helpÂ·info); born 1 October 1945) is an Indian politician serving as the 14th and current President of India since 25 July 2017.[1] He is also the first person from Uttar Pradesh to serve as President of India.[2] Prior to his presidency, he served as the 26th governor of Bihar from 2015 to 2017[3][4] and as a member of Parliament, Rajya Sabha from 1994 to 2006. Before entering politics, he was a lawyer for 16 years and practiced in the Delhi High Court and the Supreme Court of India until 1993.
